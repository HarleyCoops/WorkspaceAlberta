## Description (EN)
Parks Canada Agency (PCA) with Jasper National Park (JNP) requires the services of a Contractor for the interim rehabilitation of the H.J. Moberly Bridge in Jasper Alberta. The H.J. Moberly Bridge has reached the end of its service life and is scheduled to be replaced; however, the replacement is not a part of this project.

The Scope of Work includes, but is not limited to: 

1.	Strengthening of the two end floor beams at each main truss span, on both sides for the structure.
2.	Replacement of top timber decking (wearing surface) and sidewalk deck timber at specified locations. 
3.	Traffic accommodation will be primarily Single Lane Alternating Traffic (SLAT) during construction hours. 

TWO STAGE SELECTION PROCESS 

This posting is the Stage Two of a two-stage solicitation process: the first stage was to evaluate the qualifications of all interested suppliers. The second stage (Stage Two) is to invite the qualified suppliers to provide a financial bid on the project based on the specifications and drawings. 

Only qualified suppliers have been invited to bid.

Potential and interested sub-contractors are invited to add their company’s name under the section “Partner with another business”.

## Description (FR)
L’Agence Parcs Canada (APC), en collaboration avec le parc national Jasper (PNJ), recherche un entrepreneur pour la réhabilitation provisoire du pont H.J. Moberly à Jasper, en Alberta. Le pont H.J. Moberly a atteint la fin de sa durée de vie utile et son remplacement est prévu; toutefois, ce remplacement ne fait pas partie du présent projet.

L’étendue des travaux comprend, sans toutefois s’y limiter:

1.	Le renforcement des deux poutres d’extrémité de chaque travée principale, des deux côtés de la structure.
2.	Le remplacement du platelage supérieur (couche de roulement) et du platelage du trottoir aux endroits précis.
3.	La circulation sera principalement alternée sur une seule voie pendant les heures de travaux.
