## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Verify Your API Keys

Your workspace needs API keys to connect to your business tools. Check the terminal output above to see which keys are configured.

**To add missing keys:**
1. Click your profile picture (top-right) > Settings
2. Go to "Codespaces" > "Secrets"
3. Add the required secrets for your tools

### Step 2: Open the AI Assistant

Press `Ctrl+Shift+I` (or `Cmd+Shift+I` on Mac) to open the AI chat panel.

The AI assistant (Claude) can help you:
- Analyze your business data
- Create automations
- Build reports and dashboards
- Connect your tools together

### Step 3: Describe Your Problem

Your business problem:

> I wish AI could take a blog post we write and automatically create social media variations for each platform.

Start by asking the AI assistant to help you with this specific issue!

## Your Connected Tools

The following MCP servers are configured in this workspace:

| Tool | Requirements | What It Does |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| Filesystem | None | Read and write files in your workspace |
| Google Drive | Needs: GOOGLE_DRIVE_CREDENTIALS_JSON, GOOGLE_DRIVE_TOKEN_JSON | Files, Docs, Sheets, Slides |
| Slack | Needs: SLACK_BOT_TOKEN, SLACK_APP_TOKEN | Channels, messages, and DMs |
| Buffer | Needs: BUFFER_ACCESS_TOKEN, BUFFER_OPENAPI_URL | Social posting |
| Mailchimp | Needs: MAILCHIMP_API_KEY, MAILCHIMP_SERVER_PREFIX, MAILCHIMP_OPENAPI_URL | Email marketing |
| Google Analytics | Needs: GA_PROPERTY_ID, GA_CLIENT_EMAIL, GA_PRIVATE_KEY, GA_OPENAPI_URL | Web and product analytics |
| Calendly | Needs: CALENDLY_PERSONAL_TOKEN | Scheduling links and bookings |
